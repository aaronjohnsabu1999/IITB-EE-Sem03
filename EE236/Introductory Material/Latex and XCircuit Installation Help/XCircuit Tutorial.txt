XCircuit Tutorial

This is a simple software to
 make circuit diagrams.


Press {Ctrl + Alt + T} to launch terminal

Type {xcircuit} and press enter to launch xcircuit.


Add elements by pressing {L}

To make wire connections press {W}

To end wire connection press the {Scroll button}
Corrections can be made using options on the right hand side panel - scissors to delete, stars to copy and to move.


Other options like mirroring the component vertically or horizontally are also available on the right panel.

To label components "A" symbol has to be clicked.

Press {u} for undo
.
Save the file
 frequently.
 

Go to "File/Format Page Output"

It is better to save the circuit diagram in .eps format
Do not forget to click "Apply" before clicking on the "Write file"
