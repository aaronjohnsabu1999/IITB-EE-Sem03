* Solar Cell SPICE Subcircuit Data

.subckt solar_cell PX NX

*IL is photo generated current
*IL NX TMP dc 0e-3
*IL NX TMP dc 8e-3
IL NX TMP dc 10e-3

d1 TMP NX diode
.model diode d (is=(1e-13) n=1)

d2 TMP NX diode2
.model diode2 d (is=(2e-6) n=2)

*rs TMP PX 0
rs TMP PX 10
*rs TMP PX 30

*rsh TMP NX 5e2
*rsh TMP NX 1e3
rsh TMP NX 5e3

.ends solar_cell
